1. Introduction

Welcome to QuantDataApi ("we", "us", or "our"). We operate the QuantDataApi platform, providing financial market data APIs and developer tools. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive through our website and services.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the platform or use our API services.

2. Data Collection

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address and Full Name
  • Company name and Professional role
  • Billing information and Payment methods
  • Usage data and API request logs
  • IP addresses and browser fingerprints for security

API Usage Logs

As an API provider, we collect technical data related to your queries. This includes the endpoints accessed, timestamps, query parameters, and response times. This data is essential for billing, rate limiting, and infrastructure optimization.

3. Cookie Policy

QuantDataApi uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication: Keeping you logged into the developer console.
  • Preferences: Remembering your theme settings (e.g., Dark Mode) and API defaults.
  • Analytics: Understanding how developers navigate our documentation to improve clarity.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

4. How We Use Data

QuantDataApi uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including API uptime.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service or API versioning.
  • To provide customer support and technical troubleshooting.
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our platform.

5. Third-party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personal Data to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may employ third party companies to facilitate our Service (e.g., payment processors like Stripe, or infrastructure providers like AWS).
  • Compliance with Laws: We may disclose your data where required by law or subpoena if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with the law.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, your Personal Data may be transferred.

6. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit.
  • Granular API key permissions (Scoping).
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.

7. Your GDPR & CCPA Rights

QuantDataApi aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data. If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California (CCPA), you have certain data protection rights:

  • The right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object.
  • The right of restriction.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent.
